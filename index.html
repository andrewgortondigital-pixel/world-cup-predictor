📊 How the model works

Team strength is anchored to bookmakers' outright prices, so the market does most of the talking. Match outcomes use a Poisson goals model from each side's rating, with a small lift for the host nations at home. Knockout ties that finish level are settled by a rating-weighted shootout. The whole tournament is simulated thousands of times; a team's title chance is simply how often it lifts the trophy. My "feeling" only moves the dial on near-coin-flips — e.g. a slight bump for Brazil's and Morocco's ceilings.
